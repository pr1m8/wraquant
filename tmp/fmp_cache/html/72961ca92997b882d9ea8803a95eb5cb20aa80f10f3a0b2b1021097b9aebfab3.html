About us

FMP (Financial Modeling Prep) leverages proprietary technology to provide a suite of financial market datasets at a fraction of traditional costs, while maintaining high standards of accuracy and comprehensive coverage. Our scalable, real-time APIs deliver on-demand access to market movers, fundamental financial statements, macroeconomic indicators, ESG data, and more — helping businesses build smarter investment tools, data dashboards, and automated trading systems. Trusted by institutional investors, fintech platforms, and corporate finance leaders worldwide, FMP helps organizations make faster, data-driven decisions with confidence. From seamless API integrations to advanced historical data endpoints and regulatory-compliant coverage, we help financial innovators accelerate product development, optimize investment strategies, and scale globally. 🔹 Real-time & 30+ years of historical market data 🔹 Full financial statements & company fundamentals 🔹 Flexible subscription models & straightforward licensing 🔹 Enterprise integration & developer-first infrastructure Learn more at www.financialmodelingprep.com or connect with us to schedule a demo.

    The consensus estimate is one of the most misused numbers in finance. Not because it's wrong — but because people treat an average like it's a verdict. Two companies can show identical consensus EPS with completely different levels of analyst disagreement underneath. One estimate with a $1.00 low and a $3.00 high averages to the same $2.00 as 20 analysts who all agree. The headline looks the same. The risk doesn't. When dispersion is high, a "stable" consensus can be hiding a lot. That's where earnings surprises come from. We broke down how to actually measure it using FMP's Analyst Estimates API — link to the full guide in the comments. #FinancialAnalysis #EarningsEstimates #EquityResearch
