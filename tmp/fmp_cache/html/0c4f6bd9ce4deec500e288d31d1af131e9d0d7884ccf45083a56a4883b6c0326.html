About FMP Company Profile APIs

FMP’s Company Profile APIs deliver rich metadata on public companies, including executives, business summaries, headcount, ownership metrics, and key valuation stats. Whether you're screening for growth-stage companies or benchmarking a peer group, this dataset offers essential fundamentals beyond the financial statements.
